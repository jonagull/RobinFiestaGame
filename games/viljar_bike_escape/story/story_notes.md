# Robin Bike Escape — Story Notes

## The World
Robin works at Rubus. He lives somewhere in Bergen and bikes everywhere.
His girlfriend is Tiril.

## Story Beats

### 1. The Office Call (intro)
Guy calls Robin. Big day at Rubus, Robin needs to get in.
Robin is at home, hops on the bike.
The whole team chases him for... reasons.

**Goal:** Robin's house → Rubus office

### 2. The Nico Call
Partway through (or after arriving at Rubus?), Nico calls.
Something pulls Robin toward Tidsbanken.

**Goal:** → Tidsbanken

### 3. The Golf Course
Robin ends up at the golf course somehow.
Needs a reason — maybe Nico again, maybe someone else.

**Goal:** → Golf course

### 4. Home to Tiril
Finally Robin just wants to go home to his girlfriend Tiril.

**Goal:** → Robin's home / Tiril

## Characters
- **Robin** — protagonist, bikes everywhere
- **Guy** — calls Robin to the office, works at Rubus
- **Nico** — calls Robin mid-story, redirects him
- **Trygve** — chaser, drives a TM car
- **Are** — chaser, throws things
- **Viljar** — chaser
- **Yuval** — chaser, does the bugslide
- **Tiril** — Robin's girlfriend, final destination
- **Jonathan**, **Nils**, **Dag** — chasers
